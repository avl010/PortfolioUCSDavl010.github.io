Spotify Symphony

Spotify Symphony is a concept and prototype for a music streaming application designed exclusively for classical music enthusiasts. Using Figma, the design incorporates components from Spotify's visual language while tailoring the experience to focus on classical compositions. The layout emulates Spotify's familiar interface, but with a specialized emphasis on classical music content.

Features

One distinctive feature of Spotify Symphony is the incorporation of a unique music player resembling a vinyl player. This distinctive design element adds a touch of nostalgia and sophistication, enhancing the immersive experience for users who appreciate classical music's timeless beauty.

Problem

The prototype addresses the challenges posed by foldable screens, specifically tailored for the Galaxy Z Fold4.

Solution

The layout adapts seamlessly to the foldable screen, ensuring a responsive and engaging user experience across different device configurations. This forward-thinking approach embraces emerging technologies and devices, providing users with a cutting-edge and visually pleasing interface.
